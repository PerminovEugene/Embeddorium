Income Tax Guide

This guide explains how income tax is calculated and declared in Estonia. All residents and non-residents with Estonian-source income are required to file an annual income tax return with the Estonian Tax and Customs Board.

The income tax rate is 20% on taxable income above the basic exemption. To understand the full declaration process, read the declaration guide.

Entrepreneurs using the simplified business account should consult the simplified account page for details on applicable rates and reporting periods.

If you have questions not covered here, visit the contact page to reach a tax adviser.
